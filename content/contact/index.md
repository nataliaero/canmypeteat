We would love to hear from you! Whether you have a question about a specific food, a suggestion for a new article, or spotted something that needs correcting, do not hesitate to reach out.

## Get in Touch

**General inquiries**: hello@canmypeteat.org

**Privacy questions**: privacy@canmypeteat.org

## What We Can Help With

- **Article suggestions** — Is there a food and pet combination you want us to cover? Let us know
- **Corrections** — Spotted an error or outdated information? We take accuracy seriously and appreciate corrections
- **Partnership inquiries** — Interested in working with us? We are open to collaborations that benefit pet owners
- **Technical issues** — Something not working on the site? Please let us know so we can fix it

## What We Cannot Help With

- **Specific veterinary advice** — We are not veterinarians. If your pet is ill or has eaten something dangerous, please contact your vet or an emergency animal poison control hotline immediately
- **ASPCA Animal Poison Control**: (888) 426-4435
- **Pet Poison Helpline**: (855) 764-7661

## Response Time

We aim to respond to all emails within 48 hours. During busy periods, it may take a bit longer. We appreciate your patience.
