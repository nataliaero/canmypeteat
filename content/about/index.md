## Our Mission

We started **Can My Pet Eat?** with one simple goal: to give pet parents quick, reliable answers about what foods are safe for their animals.

We know the feeling — you're enjoying a snack and your furry friend gives you *those eyes*. Before sharing, you grab your phone and search. That's where we come in.

## What We Do

Every article on this site follows the same simple format:

- **A clear verdict** — Yes, No, or Caution — right at the top
- **Detailed explanation** of why a food is safe or dangerous
- **Serving guidelines** when the food is safe in moderation
- **Warning signs** to watch for if your pet accidentally eats something harmful

## Our Standards

We take pet safety seriously. Our content is:

- Researched using veterinary sources and peer-reviewed studies
- Reviewed and updated regularly as new information becomes available
- Written in plain, accessible language for everyone

## Important Disclaimer

We are **not veterinarians**. This site provides general information only. Every pet is unique, and what's safe for most animals may not be safe for yours. **Always consult your veterinarian** before making changes to your pet's diet, especially if your pet has health conditions or allergies.
